Jawa Latin IPA

Modified from my Indonesian-suku keyboard that support Javanese language, especially IPA for Javanese script. The main features of this keyboard are:

  1. Multi-layer: 'default'-'symbols'-'ipa' (rotating), 'shift', and 'numeric'
  2. Button rotation: for symbols and foreign letters
  3. Longpress: if the button has a dot on the top right corner, you can longpress it to access additional letters/symbols
  4. 'ny' and 'ng' bigraph letters; 'è' and 'é' vowels.
  5. All Keyman Keyboards are multi-platform: PC, Android, Mac, iOS, web, etc. If you have access to the .kmn file, you can use it anywhere.
And this keyboard try to stay faithful to the QWERTY keyboards layout that most people familiar with.

This keyboard is designed for Javanese Latin, with special support for IPA characters (long press button or press < button to access the additional layer).

The buttons are: (from top to bottom, left to right)

  • ɛ - on "è" (bebek)
  • ə & ĕ - on "e" (pepet)
  • ṛ - on "r"
  • ʈ & ṭ (th) - on "t"
  • ɲ & ñ - on "ny"
  • å - on "a"
  • ṣ & ś - (sa murda/mahaprana)
  • ʂ & ɕ - on "s"
  • ɖ & ḍ (dh) - on "d"
  • ŋ & ṅ - on "ng"
  • ʰ - on "h"
  • ʒ & dʒ - on "j"
  • ḷ - on "l"
  • ɣ ('eu' sound)- on "é"
  • ʃ & tʃ - on "c"

    • Other than that, there are several keyboard special keys:

    • Macron: 'ĀāĪīŪūṝḹ' = "--" + 'AaIiUurl'
